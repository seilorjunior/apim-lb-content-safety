# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported by contacting the project maintainer through GitHub. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

See the [full text](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) for the canonical version.
